This fountain design (above) is also a timepiece. Each of the vertical discs rotate at different rates, one every minute, another every hour, another every day, year, and decade. This motion is powered by the fountain at the center. As the water hits the spinning wheel, it turns a set of gears that power the moving discs. This is a gravity powered design that will run without electricity.
